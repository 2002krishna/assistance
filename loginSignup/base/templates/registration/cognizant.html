Cognizant

Cognizant is a multinational technology company specializing in IT services and consulting. Founded in 1994 and headquartered in Teaneck, New Jersey, Cognizant operates in over 40 countries and serves clients in various industries, including financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, and retail.

The company offers a wide range of services, including digital transformation, IT consulting, software development, and business process outsourcing. Cognizant is known for its expertise in emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and blockchain.

Cognizant is committed to helping clients navigate the digital age and achieve their business goals. The company's culture emphasizes collaboration, innovation, and customer success. Cognizant also places a strong emphasis on corporate social responsibility, supporting initiatives related to education, environmental sustainability, and community development.
