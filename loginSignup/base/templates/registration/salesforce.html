Salesforce

Salesforce is a leading cloud-based customer relationship management (CRM) platform that enables companies to connect with their customers, partners, and employees in entirely new ways. Founded in 1999 by Marc Benioff and Parker Harris, Salesforce pioneered the idea of CRM software delivered over the internet, also known as Software-as-a-Service (SaaS).

With a focus on innovation and customer success, Salesforce offers a wide range of products and services designed to help businesses of all sizes grow and thrive. Its flagship product, Salesforce Customer 360, provides a unified view of customer data, enabling companies to deliver personalized experiences across marketing, sales, commerce, service, and more.

In addition to CRM, Salesforce offers a variety of cloud-based solutions including analytics, artificial intelligence, app development, and collaboration tools. The company is committed to sustainability and social responsibility, striving to create a positive impact on society and the environment through initiatives like the Salesforce Sustainability Cloud and the 1-1-1 model of philanthropy.
