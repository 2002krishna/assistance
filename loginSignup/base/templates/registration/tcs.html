Tata Consultancy Services

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. It is a subsidiary of the Tata Group and operates in 46 countries. TCS is one of the largest Indian companies by market capitalization and is ranked among the most valuable IT services brands worldwide.

Founded in 1968, TCS offers a wide range of services including software development, infrastructure management, engineering services, and digital solutions. The company serves clients in various industries such as banking, finance, healthcare, retail, and manufacturing.

TCS is known for its commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction. It leverages cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and blockchain to deliver transformative solutions to its clients. TCS has a strong focus on corporate social responsibility and sustainability, actively contributing to community development and environmental conservation initiatives.
