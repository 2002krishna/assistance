Wipro

Wipro Limited is a leading global information technology, consulting, and business process services company. Established in 1945, Wipro operates in over 50 countries and serves clients across various industries, including banking, healthcare, retail, and telecommunications.

The company offers a comprehensive range of services, including digital strategy, application development, infrastructure management, and business process outsourcing. Wipro is known for its commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Wipro's culture is built on a foundation of values such as integrity, respect, and diversity. The company places a strong emphasis on corporate social responsibility, actively supporting initiatives related to education, environmental sustainability, and community development.
